About FLL
What is FIRST LEGO League?

FIRST LEGO League is a global robotics competition program designed to inspire young people to think like scientists and engineers. Every season, teams compete across four major areas: the Robot Game, where an autonomous LEGO robot completes missions on a themed mat; Robot Design, which evaluates the engineering and construction of the robot itself; the Innovation Project, where teams research and present a solution to a real-world problem; and Core Values, which celebrates teamwork, inclusion, and gracious professionalism both on and off the competition floor.

🤖

Robot game

We program an autonomous LEGO SPIKE robot to complete missions on a themed game mat — precision and strategy are everything.

🔧

Robot design

We engineer and build our robot from the ground up, combining smart mechanics with thoughtful design to tackle every challenge.

💡

Innovation project

Each season we identify a real-world problem, research it deeply, design a solution, and present it to judges and our community.

⭐

Core values

Discovery, Innovation, Impact, Inclusion, Teamwork, and Fun — we live these values in every meeting, tournament, and outreach event.

Outreach
Giving back to the FLL community
At Flame Bots, we believe in sharing our passion for robotics beyond the competition floor. We participate in scrimmages to help other teams prepare for the real thing, and contribute to FLL leagues to support the next generation of builders and innovators.

Scrimmage — Season 1: Submerged

Season 1: Submerged

We hosted the Screammage — a Halloween-themed practice competition held on October 27, 2024 at The Play Space in San Jose. It was a robot game only event that we organized to give other teams a feel for what the real FLL tournament experience is like.

Scrimmage — Season 2: Unearthed

Season 2: Unearthed

We hosted the Screammage — Season 2: Unearthed — a Halloween-themed practice competition held on October 26, 2025 at 350 Showers Dr, Mountain View. A robot game only event we organized to help teams sharpen their skills ahead of the season.

FLL Spring League — Season 2: Unearthed

Season 2: Unearthed

We took on a bigger role in the FLL community by participating in the FLL Spring League — Season 2: Unearthed — demonstrating our innovation project and robot to other teams, and helping them improve their game strategies and robot design.
